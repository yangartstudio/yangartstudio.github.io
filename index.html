Richard Yang

Born in Guangzhou, China in 1960, graduated from the Guangzhou Academy of Fine Arts in 1987 as an oil painter. In 1991, I was studying graphic design at Academy of Art University. Based in San Francisco and Sacramento, CA, continues to paint while working on photography. Afterwards, I became an independent artist in 1998 and began to participate in various art exhibitions and competitions. In 2010, I established Yang Art Studio. Member of Oil Painters of America, California Art Club, Signature Member of Sacramento Fine Arts Center, Member of Yolo Arts.
